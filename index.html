Pure Epoxy

Pure epoxy powders are based on combination of epoxy resins and hardeners such as DCDA and carboxylic salts of cyclic amidines. These products are recommended for interior decorativeapplications.

Epoxy Polyester

Epoxy Polyester Powders are based on a combination of saturated carboxyl terminated Polyester Resins and Epoxy Resins. These products are recommended for cost effective interior decorative applications.

Pure Polyester

Pure Polyester powders based on saturated carboxyl terminated polyester resins and TGIC.Pure Polyester powders provide an excellent, versatile coating suitable for various applications. Primarily employed in architectural contexts, Pure Polyester Powder stands out for its exceptional outdoor durability and mechanical properties. Highly recommended for exterior use, it can also find application in select interior settings where color fastness is a priority.

Applied

Essentially, powder coating is used wherever a durable, attractive, and environmentally friendly finish is desired on metal surfaces.

Powder coating paint is applied to a wide range of metal surfaces across various industries. Here's a breakdown:

Lighting Industry

Powder coating is used in the Lighting industry for Lighting fixtures, especially those used outdoors, are exposed to harsh weather conditions, UV radiation, and potential physical damage. Powder coating provides a tough, protective layer that resists chipping, scratching, fading, and corrosion, ensuring the longevity of the fixtures.

Architectural Industry

Powder coating protects building exteriors from weathering, UV radiation, and pollution, ensuring long-lasting aesthetics and reducing maintenance costs, It provides a durable and attractive finish for aluminum and steel window and door frames, enhancing the overall look of the building.

Furniture Industry

Powder coating is increasingly used in the furniture industry, offering a durable, attractive, and environmentally friendly finish, it ideal for metal furniture like chairs, tables, shelves, and frames, providing a tough, chip-resistant finish, it's offer excellent resistance to scratches, chips, fading, and corrosion, ensuring the furniture can withstand daily use and harsh environments.

Electrical Industry

Powder coating plays a crucial role in the electrical industry, offering a range of benefits for various components and equipment, It provides a durable and aesthetically pleasing finish for switchgear and control panels, enhancing their appearance and protecting them from environmental factors, it's used on transformer and motor windings to provide electrical insulation and protect them from moisture, chemicals, and abrasion

Industrial Equipment Industry

Powder coating protects machinery frames, housings, gears, and other components from corrosion, abrasion, and chemicals.

Bulldozers, excavators, and other construction equipment, and tractors, harvesters, and other agricultural machinery benefit from powder coating's resistance to weathering, chemicals, and physical damage

Household Appliances

Powder coating is used in the consumer goods industry for coating products such as Refrigerators, Washing Machines and Dryers, Dishwashers, Microwave Ovens and toys due to its durability, scratch resistance, and aesthetic appeal.
